KANERVA'S WEBPAGE ABOUT COFFEE

Kanerva works as a part time barista, so here is her quick guide about the basic coffee drinks:

  • espresso
  • cappuccino
  • flat white
  • caffe latte

Espresso

A well balanced and flavorful shot of espresso requires a well trained barista and quality ingredients. If well prepared, it is naturally sweet and womderful. However, when badly brewed it propably ruins your day.

Cappuccino

Cappuccino is served everywhere. A good cappuchino is soft, creamy and served in cup not bigger than 2dl. You should be able to taste the coffee under the milk!

Flat White

Flat white is a newer addition to the family, but as you might suspect from the name - it should be flat. A big foam of milk does not belong in your cup of flat white. You also cannot get it black. Flat black isn't a thing.

Caffe Latte

Did you know that latte means milk? So if you order a latte, you are basically ordering milk without the coffee. But sure, most baristas understand what people mean with latte. You want the coffee as well.
